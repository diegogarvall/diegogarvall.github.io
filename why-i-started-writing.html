A good way to start: pick one small, specific thought you've had recently — an opinion, an observation, something that seemed obvious to you but that most people don't say out loud. Explain what it is, then explain why you think it's true, using an example or two from your own life or something you've noticed.

You don't need a grand conclusion. You just need to follow the thought honestly until it runs out.
